Welcome to Smart Waste Collection Routing

A Modern Solution for a Cleaner India

India faces a significant challenge with its growing waste problem:
Mountains of uncollected garbage fill our cities and towns, harming public health, polluting land and water, and impacting daily life.

Efficient, smart, and optimized garbage collection is not just a convenience — it's a necessity for public health, environmental sustainability, and our collective well-being.

Today, India produces more than 1.5 lakh tonnes of solid waste daily. Without modern management and technological intervention, our cities risk being overwhelmed.
Innovative, tech-first solutions can transform our urban and rural environments, reduce costs, and inspire communities to participate in Swachh Bharat.
Our mission: Use technology and smart routing to make waste collection faster, cheaper, and greener.
